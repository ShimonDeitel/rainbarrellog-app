Privacy Policy

Rain Barrel Log is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or to any third party. We do not collect, sell, or share any personal information.

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or network tracking of any kind. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple's App Store / StoreKit; we never see your payment details.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is deleted with it.
